What is Box Breathing?

Box breathing, also known as square breathing, is a simple relaxation technique that can help you reduce stress and improve focus. It involves breathing in a pattern that forms a mental square.

How to Practice Box Breathing:

  1. Inhale slowly for 4 seconds
  2. Hold your breath for 4 seconds
  3. Exhale slowly for 4 seconds
  4. Hold your breath for 4 seconds
  5. Repeat the cycle

Practice this technique for 5-10 minutes to experience its calming effects.
